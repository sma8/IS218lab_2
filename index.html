Harry Potter

The Harry Potter series was written by J.K Rowling. There are seven books in the series: Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone, Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets, Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban, Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire, Harry Potter and the Order of Phoenix, Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince, Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows. There were eight movies made about this series. Some of the main characters are Harry Potter, Hermonie Granger, Ron Weasley, and Draco Malfoy. They go to Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. The Headmaster is Albus Dumbledore.
